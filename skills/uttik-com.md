## About Uttik

Uttik is an AI Answer Engine that helps organizations create, govern, and distribute accurate answers across websites, search engines, AI assistants, and customer support channels.

As search shifts from pages to AI-generated responses, brands risk losing control over how they are explained. Uttik solves this by providing a centralized layer of verified answers that AI systems can reliably retrieve and present.

Built for Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Uttik structures brand knowledge for accuracy, consistency, and discoverability in AI-driven search. It replaces fragmented FAQs and unreliable chatbots with a single source of truth.

Uttik is used by enterprises, e-commerce brands, SaaS companies, and regulated industries that care about trust, clarity, and operational efficiency in the age of AI answers.
